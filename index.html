About Me

Welcome to my website! I am Kartikey from Lucknow, UP. I am pursuing my masters in Computer Science at IIT Mandi.

I enjoy programming in C++ and Python. I am currently experimenting with Qt app development, influenced by KDE. My posts include my experiences and ways I solve problems.

I also enjoy drawing (on paper). It's only been around six months when I started properly learning. I mainly focus on drawing pages from manga I read. I don't have the ability or confidence to make original art just yet.

I also enjoy reading! These days I've been a lot into Japanese light novels. I also like to read about history and ideas of revolutionaries, specially from India. Visit my book list to find out my recommendations and favourites!
